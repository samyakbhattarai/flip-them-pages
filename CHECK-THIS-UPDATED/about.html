About Team Stark

Welcome to our interactive space journey! This digital flipbook takes you on an extraordinary tour through the cosmos, exploring the wonders of our universe.

Created by Team Stark, this project combines stunning visuals with engaging storytelling to bring the mysteries of space to life.

Journey Through Space

Each page reveals new celestial wonders, from distant galaxies to mysterious phenomena. Navigate through the chapters to discover the beauty and science of space exploration.

Follow the adventures of Flara and friends as they explore solar storms, aurora borealis, and the incredible teamwork between the Sun, Earth, and satellites to protect our world.

Interactive Experience

Use the arrow buttons or your keyboard's arrow keys to flip through pages. Each chapter brings you closer to understanding the amazing phenomena that occur in space weather.
